Under Graduate course : B. Sc. (Computer Science) : No. of Seats 160


Objective :


  • To develop problem solving abilities using a computer

  • To build the necessary skill set and analytical abilities for developing computer based solutions for real life problems.

  • To create awareness about process and product standards

  • To train students in professional skills related to Software Industry.

  • To prepare necessary knowledge base for research and development in Computer Science

  • To help students build-up a successful career in Computer Science

Eligibility Criteria


  • Higher Secondary School Certificate (10+2) Science stream with English and Mathematics compulsory or Three Years Diploma in Computer Science Course of Board of Technical Education conducted by Government of Maharashtra or its equivalent.


Post Graduate course : M. Sc. (Computer Science) : No. of Seats 60


Introduction


  1. The course is conducted under Choice Based Credit System. The duration of the course is two years.

  2. Course would be of 100 credits, where each course will be of 5 credits and one credit will be equivalent to 15 clock hours.

  3. Every student shall complete 100 credits in a minimum of four semesters.

  4. In fourth semester student has to complete Industrial Training for six months.


Eligibility


  • Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS) with 50% marks and 45% marks for Reserved category. OR

  • B.Sc. (Computer Science) with 50% marks. OR

  • A Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science/ Information Technology/Electronic Telecommunication with 50% marks.
