Vision


  • Our vision is to offer up to date programs which will allow our graduates to be competitive in the job market. We strive to provide a hands-on approach in teaching computer related Programs.



Mission


  • The mission of the department is to advance the knowledge and educate the students in computer science and technology that will best serve the nation and the world in the 21st

  • We seek to develop in each member of the computer science department the ability and passion to work wisely, creativity and effectively for the betterment of humankind.


Programme Outcomes :


    After successfully completing B. Sc. (Computer Science) Programme students will be able to:


  • Use creativity, critical thinking, analysis and research skills to solve theoretical and real- world problems in computer science

  • Work effectively both individually and as member of team.

  • Discuss software development fundamentals, including programming, data structures, algorithms and complexity

  • Illustrate the concepts of systems fundamentals, including architectures and organization, operating systems, networking and communication

  • Gain the knowledge about software engineering fundamentals, including software analysis and design, evaluation and testing, and software engineering processes.

  • Communicate effectively for different purposes and in different situations.

  • Gain self-discipline in everyday aspects of life and work.

  • Describe mathematics fundamentals, including discrete structures, statistics and calculus.

  • Illustrate the concepts of Microprocessors and micro controllers.

  • Make use of Application fundamentals, including information management and intelligent applications.

Programme Specific Outcomes :


  • Apply knowledge of computing and mathematics appropriate to the discipline

  • Develop problem-solving abilities using computer.

  • Design the application using programming languages.

  • Ability to understand the principles and development methodologies of computer systems.

  • Use knowledge of computer science in various computer domains
